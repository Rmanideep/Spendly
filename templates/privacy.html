Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and hashed password when you create an account.
  • Expense Data: Information you voluntarily input into the application, including amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and any attachments or notes related to your expenses.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with the Service, such as login times and feature usage, to improve our product.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To store and display your expense records as you input them.
  • To generate insights, summaries, and visualizations of your spending patterns.
  • To maintain your account and provide customer support.
  • To periodically notify you about important updates to the Service or these privacy practices.

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal or financial data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally within our secure database servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You also play a role in protecting your data by keeping your account credentials confidential and using strong passwords.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party service providers to assist in operating our platform and providing the Service, including hosting providers, analytics services, and email delivery services. These third parties have limited access to your data and may only process it on our instructions and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • You may update your account information at any time.
  • You may delete individual expense entries through the application interface.
  • You may request deletion of your entire account by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. If you choose to delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal information and expense data, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Spendly does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the contact information available on our website.
